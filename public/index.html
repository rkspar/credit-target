Our mission is to provide financial solutions, service and education enabling consumers to reduce debt, build wealth and achieve financial freedom.

We Build, Repair Your Credit

The mission of Credit Target is to take our clients by the hand and deliver first class credit repair and advanced credit repair tools to make our community more successful and profitable.

We understand that credit repair can be an unsavory process that requires precision and patience. Getting on track with your personal finances is more achievable than ever with the right support and care that’s dedicated to developing a healthy relationship with a lifetime score improvement. Our staff has twenty five years of experience handling the full spectrum of credit and will only work exceptionally hard to check, challenge, and change your credit score. Adhering to our core values of open and honest communication is key from the moment we begin, and throughout the critical process of protecting you while meeting the obligations of credit bureaus to appropriate changes.

Our vision to be a leading financial solutions provider through the power of numbers. Our community strives to be empowered and successful in increasing credit education and eliminating debt. We strive to guide you through the whole process to make significant, long-lasting lifestyle changes that can give you access to your financial dreams through credit repair that reflects you honestly.
